Oscars Fever!

The 87th Academy Awards ceremony, presented by the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences (AMPAS), honored the best films of 2014 and took place on February 22, 2015, at the Dolby Theatre in Hollywood, Los Angeles. During the ceremony, the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences presented Academy Awards in 24 categories.

Actor Neil Patrick Harris, primarily known for his performance as Barney Stinson in hit series How I Met Your Mother, hosted the ceremony for the first time.

Screening "Her" in Auditorium 1

We invite you to our first time screening in Auditorium 1. The film we will present on the big screen is Her.

We’re very happy to announce that ITU’s own Judith Simon will introduce the screening of Her this Friday with a short talk on AI. Judith does research on Philosophy of Science and Computer ethics among other subjects.

We hope that this will encourage you to look closer at the interesting views and issues with AI shown in the film. And of course, we in the ITU.Film crew are all looking very much forward to seeing you all at the screening this Friday!
